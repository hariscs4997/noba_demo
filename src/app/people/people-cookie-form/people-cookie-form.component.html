Customise Your Cookies

When using our website, you can improve the performance and your experience by allowing us to store and retrieve information on your internet browser. This information can include customised settings you have selected on the site, information about you and your device, and your preferences. If you want to read more or update your settings later, you can access our cookie policies:

Category Status
Preferences

Preference cookies are set when you make preference changes on the site and allow for your changes to be saved for a better experience. If you don’t opt-in to these cookies when you use the site, it will behave as if it is your first time visiting our site every time you use it.
Analytics

Analytics cookies help us offer you a better user experience, allowing us to count visits and determine where users such as yourself are coming from. These cookies help us make choices of how we improve the performance of our website when we make changes and updates. If you don’t opt-in, it becomes more difficult to identify performance enhancements.
Targeting

Targeting cookies are used by us and our partners to build a profile of our users. They are used to provide information on how users use our products and provides a view into how a user comes to register for those products. Our partners collect information to build a profile based on your interests to serve more relevant ad content. If you do not opt-in to these cookies, you will not be tracked as an individual user and will be served as an anonymous user and see ads that are not relevant to you and your preferences.
Strictly Necessary

Strictly necessary cookies are required for the operation of our site and cannot be turned off. They are usually only set in response to actions you make on the website, such as selecting privacy settings, logging into an account, or requesting services. If you manually block or delete these cookies, sections of the site will not function properly. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.
